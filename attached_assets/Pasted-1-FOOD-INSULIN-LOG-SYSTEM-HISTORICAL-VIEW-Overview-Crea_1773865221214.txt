1. FOOD + INSULIN LOG SYSTEM (HISTORICAL VIEW)

🔹 Overview

Create a multi-layer logging system that allows users to track and review:
	•	Blood Glucose (BG)
	•	Carbohydrates
	•	Insulin doses
	•	Meals (with optional photo)
	•	Notes (exercise, stress, illness, etc.)

⸻

🔹 Time-Based Views

The log should support:

📅 Daily View
	•	Timeline of the day (chronological)
	•	Each entry shows:
	•	Time
	•	BG reading
	•	Carbs consumed
	•	Insulin dose
	•	Meal photo (if available)
	•	Notes

⸻

📆 Weekly View
	•	7-day summary (Mon–Sun)
	•	Each day shows:
	•	Avg BG
	•	Total carbs
	•	Total insulin
	•	of highs / lows
	•	Tap into any day → opens full daily timeline

⸻

🗓️ Monthly View
	•	Calendar layout
	•	Each day displays:
	•	Avg BG (color coded)
	•	Total carbs
	•	Total insulin
	•	Tap a date → opens daily log

⸻

📈 Yearly View
	•	Monthly breakdown (Jan–Dec)
	•	Each month shows:
	•	Avg BG
	•	Avg carbs/day
	•	Avg insulin/day
	•	Tap a month → opens monthly view

⸻

🔹 Filtering Options

User can filter logs by:
	•	Time range (custom dates)
	•	High/low events only
	•	Meals only
	•	Insulin only

⸻

🔹 Export Feature (VERY IMPORTANT)
	•	Export logs as PDF or CSV
	•	Used for doctor visits
	•	Includes:
	•	Charts
	•	Daily summaries
	•	Trend insights

⸻

📈 2. PREDICT PAGE – ADVANCED ANALYTICS

⸻

🔹 Time Range Selector

Add toggle buttons:
	•	1 Month
	•	3 Months
	•	6 Months
	•	9 Months
	•	12 Months

⸻

🔹 Metrics to Display

For each selected range, calculate:

🩸 Blood Glucose
	•	Average BG
	•	% Time in Range (70–180)
	•	% High
	•	% Low

⸻

🍽️ Carbs
	•	Avg carbs per day
	•	Total carbs
	•	Peak carb times (patterns)

⸻

💉 Insulin
	•	Avg insulin per day
	•	Total insulin used
	•	Avg per meal

⸻

🔹 Charts (VERY IMPORTANT)

Each metric should have:
	•	Line chart (trend over time)
	•	Smooth curve (not jagged)
	•	Highlight:
	•	High points
	•	Low points
	•	Stable zones

⸻

🧠 3. A1C ESTIMATION (KEY FEATURE)

⸻

🔹 Formula

Use standard estimated A1C formula:

👉 A1C = (Average BG + 46.7) ÷ 28.7

⸻

🔹 Display

Show:
	•	Estimated A1C for selected range
	•	Label:
	•	✅ Good (below 7)
	•	⚠️ Needs Attention (7–8)
	•	🚨 High Risk (8+)

⸻

🔹 Trend Insight (THIS IS 🔥)

Example messages:
	•	“Your A1C has improved over the last 3 months.”
	•	“Frequent evening highs are impacting your A1C.”
	•	“Improving post-meal control could lower your A1C.”

⸻

🚨 4. SMART INSIGHTS ENGINE

⸻

🔹 Pattern Detection

System should automatically detect:
	•	Repeated highs after meals
	•	Nighttime lows
	•	Morning spikes

⸻

🔹 Suggestions (Dynamic)

Examples:

High Trends:
	•	“You tend to spike after dinner. Consider pre-meal insulin timing.”

Low Trends:
	•	“Frequent lows detected overnight. Consider adjusting evening insulin.”

Stable:
	•	“Your glucose has been stable. Keep up the current routine.”

⸻
